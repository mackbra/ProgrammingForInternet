Ariana Grande’s “7 Rings” Reaches 5 Weeks At #1 On Pop Radio Chart

Ariana Grande’s smash hit “7 rings” predictably — and convincingly — retains its position atop the Mediabase pop radio airplay chart.

Played ~17,527 times during the March 31-April 6 tracking period, the “thank u, next” single spends a fifth consecutive week at #1.

In reaching five weeks atop the chart, “7 rings” ties “Side To Side (featuring Nicki Minaj)” as the longest-reigning pop radio #1 of Grande’s career.

This week’s spin count trails last week’s mark by 373 but keeps “7 rings” well ahead of the pack.

Ellie Goulding & Diplo’s “Close To Me (featuring Swae Lee),” which moves up one place to #2, received ~14,951 tracking week spins (+465).

Halsey’s “Without Me” slips one place to #3, while Khalid’s “Better” climbs one place to #4. Benny Blanco, Halsey & Khalid’s “Eastside” concurrently drops one level to #5.
